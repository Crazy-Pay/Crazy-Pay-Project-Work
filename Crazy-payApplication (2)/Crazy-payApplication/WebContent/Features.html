Features



Crazy-pay Mobile app is mobile banking application with enhanced features offered by Crazy-pay private limited. It is available for all leading mobile OS (Android, Apple iOS, Windows Mobile, Blackberry). This new app is customized keeping in view your banking requirements. Crazy-pay Mobile app is Smart, Simple and Secure application. All frequently required banking services can be accessed through Crazy-pay Mobile app. Users can access most of the banking services anywhere any time through internet enabled handsets. Pre login features are accessible to all without registration. Post login features can be accessed by customers of Crazy-pay private limited after completing one time registration process.



    Pre Login Features:

  • Contact details of Admin Offices.
  • Interest rates for Time Deposits
  • Missed Call service for getting Account Balance or last few Transactions over SMS.
  • Apply for new Saving Account, Retail Loan or Credit Card.
  • Real time Notifications/Alerts regarding new launch/offers/modification in schemes etc.


    Post Login Features:

  • Account Balance Enquiry/Account Details/Mini Statement.
  • Fund transfer to accounts with Banks.
  • Utility Bill payments.
  • Open & Close Time Deposit Account.
  • Registration for getting Account Statement over Email.
  • Cheque Book related functions.
  • Link Account to Aadhaar Number.
  • Request for ATM (Debit) Card & Credit Card Blocking.
  • Request for personalized ATM (Debit) Card.
